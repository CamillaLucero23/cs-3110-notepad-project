Note Taking Tips!

  • Summarize in Your Own Words: Avoid copying verbatim; instead, rewrite in a way that makes sense to you.
  • Use Abbreviations & Symbols: Speed up your note-taking by using shortcuts like “&” for "and" or arrows (→) for direction.
  • Review & Revise Regularly: Go over your notes frequently to reinforce learning and fill in gaps.
  • Limit Distractions: When taking notes, focus on the task and minimize unnecessary multitasking.
